Frequently Asked Questions - Gemini Live TTS

Is my data safe?

Yes, as long as you do not submit any sensitive, confidential, or personal information.

Read below for details.

When do you collect data?

Data is collected only when the "Transcribe with Gemini" context menu button or the extension screenshot button is pressed.

What data do you collect?

You control what data is collected. Only data contained within your text selection or image selection is collected.

Where does my data go?

Your data is sent to Google to be used by their Gemini Multimodal Live API service.

Who can see my data?

Only you and Google can see your data.

Your data is encrypted before it leaves your device and is decrypted after Google receives your data.

How does Google use my data?

Data you send to the Gemini Multimodal Live API is subject to the Gemini API Terms of Service. All data sent to Google is subject to Google's Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.

If you are using the Gemini API's "Free of charge" plan,

  • your data may be used to "provide, improve, and develop Google products and services";
  • in doing so, other humans may see the data you send;
  • therefore, Google says to not submit sensitive, confidential, or personal information while using the free plan.

How can I improve my personal privacy and data security?

Do not submit any sensitive, confidential, or personal information and activate a Cloud Billing account.

Can Google use my data in a way that better respects my privacy?

Yes.

Expand for details.

According to the Gemini API Terms of Service, Google handles submitted data differently depending on whether the data is being used for a free (that is, "unpaid") service or "paid" service. While data used for their free services may be used by Google for their own benefit, data used for paid services are not. Data used for paid services is handled more strictly according to a separate business agreement; outside of handling the data in the ways necessary to provide the Gemini API service, data is examined for legal or regulatory purposes.

How do I get Google to use my data more respectfully without paying them?

Activate a Cloud Billing account.

Expand for details.

According to the Gemini API Terms of Service:

When you activate a Cloud Billing account, all use of Gemini API and Google AI Studio is a "Paid Service" with respect to how Google Uses Your Data, even when using Services that are offered free of charge, such as Google AI Studio and unpaid quota of Gemini API. [emphasis added]
You are only charged for use of the Gemini API when your usage goes above the unpaid quota. As of the time of writing (Feb 2025), the unpaid quota provided by Gemini API is:
  • up to 10 requests per minute (that is, up to 1 transcription per 6 seconds every minute),
  • up to 1,500 requests per day (that is, up to 1 transcription per minute every day),
  • up to 4,000,000 input tokens per minute (that is, up to 5 full textbooks every minute),
  • and no quota for model output.
The unpaid quota is more than enough for the typical use of this extension, so it is possible to have your data handled as if it was under their "paid service" terms without spending any money.

How do I activate a Cloud Billing account?

First, identify the "Project name" that your API key is under by going to Google AI Studio.

If a Cloud Billing account is not activated and linked to that project, the Plan should say "Free of charge".

If you do not have an existing Cloud Billing account, follow the Google Cloud guide "Add a new Cloud Billing account".

Enable billing for the project your API key is under by following the Google Cloud guide "Enable billing for an existing project"

Finally, open the Google AI Studio page again. If the Plan says "Paid" or "Tier 1", then you have successfully activated and linked a Cloud Billing account to that project.

Is my data safe even under the Paid Plan?

Google promises to handle your data safely and securely no matter what plan or service you use, paid or unpaid. But, in the end, it is up to you to keep your data safe. Follow best practices for digital privacy and data security! Avoid submitting any sensitive, confidential, or personal information whenever possible!

Usage Tips

Text to Speech:

Select any text on a webpage, right-click, and choose "Transcribe with Gemini" from the context menu.

Image to Speech:

Click the extension icon in your address bar to activate the image selection tool.

Click and drag to select an area of the page to describe.

Playback Controls:

Click the extension icon in your toolbar to open the playback control popup.
